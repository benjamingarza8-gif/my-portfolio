3D-Printed LED Light

Tools: SOLIDWORKS, 3D Printer, LED Lights, Soldering



Project Overview

Buying an LED light from a store can be expensive and restrictive in terms of the lack of variety. My goal was to design, develop, and manufacture a repeatable process that is cost-effective and personalizable. Through the autotrace feature, an image can be imported into SOLIDWORKS and rapidly turned into a 2D sketch with minimal cleanup. After extruding the sketch and shelling to the thickness of your LED you have a channel that will illuminate the profile of your desired shape.



Key Achievements/Takeaways

  • Assembled an Ender 3 3D printer, troubleshot, tuned, and installed auto bed leveling
  • Designed and manufactured a casing for LED lights referencing an album cover
  • Soldered and connected LED light to power cable
