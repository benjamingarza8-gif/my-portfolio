Capstone: Naval Surface Warfare Center - Crane Division

Tools: ANSYS Icepak, SOLIDWORKS, Microsoft Office, Metal Fabrication, Electronics



Project Overview

The objectives were to develop a CFD analysis of a thermal system between a MOSFET and heatsink while remaining within 10% error of experimental analysis. After analyzing the data, the task was to improve the heatsink to reduce the temperature of the MOSFET. The final objective was to study the effects of convection on temperature within the system.



Key Achievements/Takeaways

  • Modeled a transient CFD analysis of the heat transfer across the MOSFET while remaining within 10% error of experimental data
  • Determined which heatsink would be the best at dissipating heat through calculations, simulation, and experimentation
  • Analyzed effects of convection on heat transfer through natural and forced convection, ultimately concluding that forced convection provided a 12% drop in the system’s ambient temperature
